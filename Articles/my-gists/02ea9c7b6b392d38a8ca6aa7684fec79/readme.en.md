# JTC DOC

The [JTC DOC](https://theme.typora.io/doc/ja/Write-Custom-Theme/) (Japanese Traditional Company Documents style) theme has been released.

This new theme is inspired by the traditional style of business documents in Japan. The JTC DOC theme offers a simple yet sophisticated formatting for creating traditional Japanese business documents.

Key features of the new theme include:

- **Beautiful Japanese Fonts**: The JTC DOC theme adopts standard Japanese fonts to enhance the visual appeal of Japanese text.

- **Traditional Formatting**: The theme adjusts paragraph styles, headings, lists, and more to align with the traditional style of business documents.

- **Ease of Use**: Combined with Typora's user-friendly interface, it makes editing and formatting Japanese text effortless and visually pleasing.

The JTC DOC theme is designed for users who need to create Japanese corporate documents and reports, and it is the result of collaboration among members of the Typora community.

For more details and instructions on how to install this theme, please visit the [JTC DOC Theme Documentation](https://theme.typora.io/doc/ja/Write-Custom-Theme/).

We hope you enjoy this new theme!
